...Welcome To Snowfall Of India...

India's best snow destinations include the famous ski hubs of Gulmarg, Auli, and Manali for winter sports, while Shimla, Kufri, and Dalhousie offer classic hill station charm with guaranteed snow. For dramatic landscapes, Ladakh boasts frozen lakes and valleys, while Tawang and parts of Sikkim (like Lachung) provide stunning, quieter snow experiences, perfect for seeing snow from December to February.
